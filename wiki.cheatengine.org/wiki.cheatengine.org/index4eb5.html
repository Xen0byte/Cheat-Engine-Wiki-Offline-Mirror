IRC info

WHAT IS IRC? internet relay chat
WHAT IS IRC FOR? instant and offtopic chat when forums are slow
WHERE IS THE SERVER? irc://irc.p2p-network.net/cef
WHAT IS THE CHANNEL? #cef
DO YOU OFFER SSL? yes, connect on ports 6697 or 7000
DO WE HAVE A WEBCHAT? #CEF WEB IRC
WHERE CAN I GET AN IRC CLIENT? mIRC, XChat or irssi

IRC Rules

     1. Identify with your CEF Username.
     2. Do not speak of your forum bans/warns on the channel and vice-versa.
     3. Do not ask for special privileges or exemptions.
     4. Do not interfere with the workings of the channel bots.
     5. Do not attempt to evade bans, mutes, or other punishments.
     6. Do not attempt to escalate or override the powers that be.
     7. Do not attempt to override or bypass the democratic bot process.
     8. Do not attempt to impersonate other IRC or CEF members.
     9. Do not abuse operator status, by sharing it with the unauthorized.
     10. Do not ask another op to unban you. Bans should be brought up with the op who banned you.
     11. Do not help or encourage others in breaking the rules above.


Not all of CEF rules apply to IRC. Cracking accounts, infecting computers, etc isn't allowed. If you feel you have been wrongfully banned by a halfop (+h), send any of the irc moderators a log and it will be investigated. Do not fake a log to get unbanned, we will know if you do!

F.A.Q.

HOW DO I JOIN THE SERVER AND CHANNEL WHILE IN IRC?

/server irc.p2p-network.net
/join #cef

HOW CAN I SECURE MY NICKNAME? By registering it. Register with the following syntax: /msg NickServ REGISTER MyPassword [email protected] You will recieve a confirmation code and further instructions by e-mail, and if you did everything correctly, you now can give others the assurance that you are who you claim to be with: /msg NickServ IDENTIFY MyPassword

CAN I COMPLAIN ABOUT MY BAN? No! If you were banned on the forums, You will PM the moderator that banned you when you ban expires. Do not elongate your ban by creating a secondary account. If you were banned on IRC, do not use this thread to complain, instead, send a private message to the user that banned you on IRC.
